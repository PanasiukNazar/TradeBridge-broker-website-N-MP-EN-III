Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit our website or engage in partner broker activities with us. Please read this policy carefully to understand how we handle your data.

1. General Provisions

We take the protection of your personal data very seriously and treat your data confidentially in accordance with applicable data protection regulations. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have regarding your data.

2. Collection of Information

We collect various types of information to provide you with better services:

2.1 Personal Data

When you visit our website or engage in partner broker activities, we may collect personal data such as your name, address, email address, and payment information. This data is required to process your inquiries and provide our services.

2.2 Automatically Collected Data

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect information about your computer, including your IP address, the browser you are using, and access times. This data helps us analyze and improve the usage of our website.

3. Use of Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes, including:

  • To process and manage your inquiries related to partner broker services;
  • To provide you with information about our partner broker activities;
  • To improve our customer service;
  • To analyze and optimize the use of our website;
  • To send you marketing communications, if you have consented to them.

4. Sharing of Your Data

We do not share your personal data with third parties without your explicit consent, except where necessary to fulfill our contractual obligations or comply with legal requirements. Possible recipients of your data include:

  • Service providers assisting with partner broker activities;
  • Payment service providers for payment processing;
  • IT service providers supporting the management of our website.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance the user experience. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us gather information about your usage of the website and store your preferences. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but please note that some features of the website may no longer be available.

6. Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. This includes encryption of data, the use of firewalls, and regular review of our security measures.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request information about the personal data we store about you at any time. You also have the right to:

  • Request correction of inaccurate data;
  • Request the deletion of your personal data;
  • Request restriction of the processing of your data;
  • Object to the processing of your personal data;
  • Request data portability.

To exercise your rights, please contact us using the contact information provided on our website.

8. Links to Third Parties

Our website may contain links to external websites operated by third parties. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these websites. We recommend that you review the privacy policies of the respective sites before providing personal data.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page and will take effect immediately. We recommend that you review this policy regularly to stay informed of any updates.

10. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy or our data protection practices, you can contact us through the contact page on our website.

Thank you for visiting our website. Your trust is important to us, and we are committed to protecting and respecting your personal data.
